Scope of the Project

Our analysis includes the top emitting industries for 2012 in the Greater Los Angeles Area because they cumulatively account for nearly 7 million pounds, or 89% of total releases in the county. The top four industries from greatest contribution to total toxic releases of Los Angeles County include: Primary Metals (38%), Petroleum (31%), Fabricated Metals (15%), and Chemicals (5%).
